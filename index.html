Emily's Big Back Adventures

An online log of my favorite dishes from restaraunts a really like going to.

Sake Toro from Umami Japanese Bistro

I might be biased because I work there, but this appetizer is so good.

  • Although, this dish can be rather pricy it is worth it.
  • Sake Toro means "salmon belly", which is the more tender part of the fish.
  • The sauce on it is a korean sweet and spicy vinegrette, which is what makes the dish so good.

Curry from Thai Pepper

In my opinion, Thai Pepper has the best curry in Baton Rouge.

  • I usually order the red curry with chicken, I think this image is of yellow curry.
  • Thai spice is on another level so if you enjoy hot foods, you should try it.
  • Thai pepper also has really good desserts, seasonally they'll have mango sticky rice and its definitely one of my favorites.

Ube ice cream & Taiyaki from Sweet Society

Baton Rouge is definitely lacking in dessert spots, but Sweet Society is a nice little spot.

  • Sweet Society has a very cute aesthetic inside and it is attached to Boru, which is a ramen place.
  • Their menu has a good variety with seasonal ice cream flavors, taiyaki, boba and bingsu.
  • It's location is a bit strange though, in the Electric Depot off of Government, but theres some good spots off of Government.
